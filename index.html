About Mycotoxin Protocols

Mycotoxins are toxic compounds produced by mold and fungi that can accumulate in the body from environmental exposure (water-damaged buildings) or contaminated food sources. These toxins can cause a wide range of symptoms including:

  • Neurological: Brain fog, memory issues, headaches, mood disorders
  • Respiratory: Chronic cough, asthma-like symptoms, sinusitis
  • Digestive: GI distress, food sensitivities, inflammation
  • Systemic: Fatigue, immune dysfunction, chronic inflammation

Effective mycotoxin treatment requires comprehensive detoxification protocols that address drainage, binding, antimicrobial support, and restoration. These protocol generators provide evidence-based, phase-specific treatment plans customized to your lab findings and patient demographics.
